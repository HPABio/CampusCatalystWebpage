Below is a **style guide** for the Campus Catalyst website inspired by the visuals you shared: **neon acid-lime + deep black**, bold sans typography, **grunge brush textures**, high-contrast “sticker” UI cards, and sharp diagonal accents.

---

## Brand vibe

**Keywords:** Electric • Experimental • Urban biotech • Night-mode • High-contrast • Bold • “Startup energy”
**Design principle:** *Minimal layout + loud accents.* Keep pages mostly black/neutral, then “hit” with neon and texture.

---

## Color system

### Core palette

* **Acid Lime (Primary):** `#8FFF2B` (formerly `#C8FF00`)
* **Pitch Black (Base):** `#0A0A0A`
* **Charcoal (Surface):** `#141414`
* **Graphite (Borders):** `#242424`
* **Off-White (Text):** `#F2F2F2`

### Supporting accents (use sparingly)

* **Soft Lime Tint:** `#E7FF8A` (hover glows, highlights)
* **Danger / Alert Red:** `#FF3B3B` (only for errors or urgency)
* **Neutral Gray:** `#B5B5B5` (secondary text)

### Gradients & glow

* Glow shadow for lime elements:

  * `0 0 24px rgba(143,255,43,0.25)`
* Subtle background gradient option:

  * Black → Charcoal radial vignette (center lighter, edges darker)

---

## Typography

### Font pairing (closest to the references)

**Option A (free + modern, strong):**

* Headings: **Space Grotesk** (700–800)
* Body/UI: **Inter** (400–600)

**Option B (more “tech/editorial”):**

* Headings: **Satoshi** / **General Sans**
* Body/UI: **Inter**

### Type scale (desktop)

* H1: 56–72px / 0.95–1.05 line-height (tight)
* H2: 40–48px
* H3: 28–32px
* Body: 16–18px
* Small: 13–14px
* Overline / label: 12px, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.08–0.12em

### Typography rules

* Use **big, loud headings** with short phrases.
* Highlight 1–3 words per headline in **Acid Lime**.
* Keep body copy clean and readable (off-white, no neon paragraphs).

---

## Layout & grid

### Grid

* 12-column layout desktop
* Max width: **1200–1280px**
* Gutters: 24px
* Section padding: 88–120px (desktop), 56–72px (mobile)

### Composition

* Black space is your “oxygen.”
* Use **asymmetric blocks** + **diagonal shapes** for energy.
* Alternate sections between:

  * clean (minimal) and
  * textured (brush/grunge background)

---

## Surfaces, borders, and shapes

### Cards (signature UI element)

* Background: Charcoal `#141414`
* Border: 1px Graphite `#242424`
* Corner radius: **20–28px** (big rounded like in references)
* Internal padding: 20–28px

### Accent shapes

* Diagonal lime wedges, corner slashes, or “cut-out” triangles
* Brush stroke overlays for “street / experimental” texture

### Texture style

* Use **grunge brush strokes** in black-on-lime or lime-on-black
* Keep them behind text at low opacity (8–18%)

---

## Iconography & graphics

### Icons

* Simple, bold, geometric (outline or solid)
* Default icon color: Off-white
* Use lime only for emphasis (selected/active states)

### Graphic motifs (from references)

* **X / strike mark** as a recurring pattern (background or separators)
* Scribbles/marker underlines for emphasis
* Sticker-like labels and tags

---

## Imagery direction

### Photos

* Prefer **black & white** or desaturated photos
* Add lime overlay elements (frames, corner tabs, callouts)
* Avoid busy full-color photos unless they’re heavily graded dark

### Speaker headshots

* Consistent crops
* Option: grayscale portraits with lime name tags

---

## Buttons & CTAs

### Primary button

* Background: Acid Lime
* Text: Black
* Radius: 999px (pill) or 18px (soft-square)
* Hover:

  * Slight scale (1.02)
  * Glow shadow

### Secondary button

* Background: transparent
* Border: 1px graphite
* Text: Off-white
* Hover: border → lime, subtle glow

### “Sticker CTA”

A special CTA style (use for key moments like “Register”):

* Lime card with grunge brush texture faintly
* A diagonal corner cut or lime wedge

---

## Navigation

### Header

* Sticky, translucent black background:

  * `rgba(10,10,10,0.75)` with blur
* Active link underline: lime brush stroke or angled bar
* Right side: **Register** button always visible

---

## Timeline section (5 steps)

### Visual style

* Dark track line + lime progress segments
* Each step = rounded card
* Step label style:

  * `01 / 02 / 03 / 04 / 05` as a large faint numeral in background
* Add small lime “marker dots” with glow

### Content pattern per step

* Step name (short, punchy)
* Date window
* Theme (1 line)
* CTA (View edition / Register)

---

## Motion & interaction

### Transitions (fast + snappy)

* 150–220ms for hovers
* Ease: `cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.8, 0.2, 1)`

### Microinteractions

* Hover glow on lime elements
* Slight parallax movement on textured backgrounds
* Timeline step highlights on scroll

Keep motion subtle; neon already creates intensity.

---

## Copy style (microcopy tone)

**Short, energetic, confident.**
Use verbs and action language:

* “Join the ecosystem.”
* “Meet founders and VCs.”
* “Turn science into impact.”

Avoid corporate fluff. Keep it sharp.

---

## Accessibility notes (important with neon)

* Don’t use lime text on white/bright backgrounds.
* For long text, use off-white on black; lime only for highlights.
* Ensure button contrast: lime-on-black or black-on-lime.
